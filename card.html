Sea Otter

Sea Otters like to eat sea urchins, clams, mussels and crabs...

  • Scientific Name: Enhydra lutris
  • Average Length: 4.5 Feet(1.4M)
  • Average Lifespan: 15 Years
  • Habitat: Pacific Coasts (Russia, Alaska, and California)

Sea Otters are the coolest animals on the planet. They lay around on their backs in the water all day, which is what 99% of humans wish they could be doing right now! They also have great personalities and seem kind for a creature that gets eaten by Great White Sharks. A Sea Otter is my spirit animal.
